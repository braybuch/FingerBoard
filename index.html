FingerBoard
Handheld Predictive Text Beta Preview

FingerBoard is a predictive text keyboard. Instead of typing the letter you press the button the letter belongs to.

This web app is the tutorial. It teaches you the basics and helps build muscle memory.

Work through the tutorial, then practice at your own pace. Your stats are tracked as you improve.

The full QWERTY keyboard was split into 4 groups

As you press buttons, the system finds all words that match that sequence.

After a few presses, usually just one word fits. Press Next to page through matches if the suggestion isn't right after you've finished typing.

During the tutorial the button you should press will be highlighted in green. But once you finish the tutorial, you're on your own!
